Banana Bread Recipe

Description

Most delicious and most banana bread you will ever eat.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven.
